    • Recipe #1

    • Time: 15
    • Serves: 6
    Ingredients Quantity
    Almond flour 1 cup
    Butter 4 Teaspoons
    Egg 3
    Cream Cheese 1/2 cup
    Stevia 2 packets
    Vanilla Extract 3 drops
    Oil 1 Teaspoon

    Instructions

    Step 1: Melt the butter in the microwave for 30 seconds.
    Step 2: Melt the cream cheese in the microwave for 30 seconds.
    Step 3: Mix all the ingredients together in a steel bowl with a rubber spatula.
    Step 4: let it sit for 5 minutes.
    Step 5: heat the pan for 1 min on medium low till the surface gets hot.
    Step 6:Pour a small amount of pancake into the pan and make it as round as possible.
    Step 7: flip over the pancake about 1 and 1/2 min each side when brown.
    Step 8: Repeat the steps.
